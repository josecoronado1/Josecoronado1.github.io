Almon Green Salad

"Fresh and Crispy"

Ingredients


1/2 pound of bacon
2 tablespoons white wine vinegar
3 tablespoons honey
1/2 tablespoons dry hot mustard
1/2 tablespoons celery salt
1/2 tablespoons ground paprika
1/4 cup olive oil
1 head red leaf lettuce tron into bite-size pieces
1 (15 ounce) can of mandarin oranges, drained
1 bunch green onions, diced
3/4 cup silvered almonds

Directions

1. In medium skillet over medium-heat, cook bacon until evenly browned. Drain cool, and crumble.

2. To make the dressing, thoroughly blend the vinegar, honey, dry mustard, celery salt, paprika, and olive oil.

2. Place lettuce, oranges, green onions, bacon, and almonds in serving bowl. Toss with dressing and serve.
